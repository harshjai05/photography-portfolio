The Eye
Behind the Lens

My name is Harsh Jaishanker. Photography, for me, is less about sharp capture and more about what slips through.

I move between street, portrait, landscape, and architecture, not as genres, but as excuses to stay a little longer than I should.

Still learning. Always noticing.
