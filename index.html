Images are managed via data.json. To add a new photo:

  1. Create a folder called photos/ next to your index.html
  2. Place your images in it (JPG/WebP recommended, under 1MB each)
  3. Open data.json and add an entry to the images array:

{ "id": 9, "src": "photos/street_03.jpg", "title": "My Photo Title", "caption": "Street, Chennai", "category": "street", "date": "2025-04-05", "featured": false }

About

The Eye
Behind the Lens

My name is Harsh Jaishanker. Photography, for me, is less about sharp capture and more about what slips through.

I move between street, portrait, landscape, and architecture — not as genres, but as excuses to stay present in a place a little longer than I should.

Based in Chennai. Still learning. Always noticing.
